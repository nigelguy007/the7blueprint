Privacy Policy

Your information, kept simple.

Last updated: 8 May 2026

This page explains what data mobiusinfinity007 collects, why we collect it, and what we do (and don't do) with it.

1. What we collect

When you use the assessment, we collect:

  • Your date of birth and full name — used solely to compute your blueprint. Stored only in your browser unless you choose to unlock the full report.
  • Your email address (only if you choose to unlock the full report) — used to deliver your blueprint and occasional related tips.
  • Your questionnaire responses — used to compute your blueprint. Treated as personal data.
  • Standard server logs (IP address, user agent, page visited) — used for security and service stability.

2. What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to third parties.
  • We don't share your DOB or name with anyone.
  • We don't use your data for advertising profiling.

3. Service providers

We use a small number of trusted services to operate the site:

  • Hosting — Vercel or Netlify (your choice as the operator), to serve the site.
  • Email delivery — Formspree, ConvertKit, or similar email service providers, when you opt in to receive your full report.
  • Payments — Stripe, if you purchase the Implementation Pack. Stripe handles all payment data; we never see or store your card information.

4. Cookies and local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember whether you've unlocked your full report, so we don't ask twice. We do not set tracking cookies. If you add analytics later, this section should be updated to reflect that.

5. Your rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time. Email hello@the7blueprint.com and we'll remove your records within 30 days.

6. Children

This service is not directed at children under 16. If we learn that we've collected information from a child under 16, we'll delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top whenever this policy changes. Material changes will be communicated by email if you've subscribed.

8. Contact

Questions: hello@the7blueprint.com
